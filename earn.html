How LP Works

When you deposit USDC, you receive LP shares representing your proportional ownership of the vault. The vault is the counterparty to all trades.

Fee income: 80% of all trading fees flow to the vault, increasing the value of your shares over time.

Risk: If traders are collectively profitable, the vault value decreases. Historically, most retail perp traders lose money, making LPs net profitable.

Cooldown: After depositing, you must wait 1 hour before withdrawing. This prevents sandwich attacks on the share price.
