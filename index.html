Architecture

Built Different

Power perps have been tried before. They failed because of infrastructure, not math. Superps fixes every failure mode.

Peer-to-Pool

No market makers, no orderbook. A USDC vault takes the other side of every trade. Liquidity is passive, always-on, and doesn't depend on third parties showing up.

Oracle-Priced

Every trade fills at the on-chain oracle price. No AMM slippage, no price discovery lag, no stale quotes. The index is always correct by construction.

LP Risk Management

The vault actively manages its exposure. LPs deposit USDC, earn fees and funding, and the protocol handles directional risk natively — no separate strategy vaults needed.

Native to HyperEVM

Sub-cent gas, 2-second blocks, and direct access to Hyperliquid's $357B/month native order book liquidity. No bridges, no L2 sequencer risk.

Multi-Asset Ready

The architecture is asset-agnostic. ETH², BTC², SOL², Gold², Silver² — each new market plugs into the same vault. Deeper shared liquidity, ~$0.65 to deploy.

Near-Zero Fixed Costs

$1.75 to deploy the entire protocol. $5–40/month to run. Cash-flow positive from trade one. No VC-subsidized liquidity mining required.
