Risk Disclosure

Superps is an experimental DeFi protocol. Smart contracts are immutable and have not been professionally audited by an external firm. Use at your own risk.

Power perpetuals have a squared payoff — this amplifies both gains and losses. A 50% HYPE spot drop creates a ~75% index drop. Combined with leverage, losses can exceed your initial collateral.

Positions below the maintenance margin (15%) are liquidated automatically. Funding payments accrue continuously and can erode collateral over time.
